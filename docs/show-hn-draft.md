Show HN: Vaulted – Local-first net worth tracker, no account, no Plaid

## URL
https://vaultedworth.com

## Text (optional, for context)
I built Vaulted because I wanted to track my net worth without linking my bank to Plaid or creating an account somewhere.

It's a Next.js static export that runs entirely in your browser:

- Data stored in IndexedDB + localStorage (never sent to a server)
- No account, no email, no bank login
- Multi-currency (USD, EUR, GBP, BRL, etc.)
- Monthly snapshots with trend charts
- JSON export/import for backups
- PWA — works offline after first load

The trade-off is manual entry (~2 min/month). For net worth tracking specifically, I think that's a reasonable price for not sharing your financial data with a third party.

Source: https://github.com/douglascorrea/vaulted
